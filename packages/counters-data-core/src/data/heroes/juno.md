## Core Playbook
Juno uses Glide Boost and Hyper Ring to stay mobile with her team's dive, healing on the move with Pulsar Torpedoes and clearing crowd control with Orbital Ray during a fight.

## Tactical Caveats
Her healing is spread thin across a wide moving group rather than concentrated, so a single ally under focused burst fire can still die despite her being nearby.
