## Core Playbook
Junkrat lobs Frag Launcher grenades around corners and into choke points to chip away at grouped enemies, using Concussion Mine for burst damage or self-mobility when a fight turns bad.

## Tactical Caveats
His projectiles are slow and highly visible, so heroes with clean sightlines or fast strafe speed can dodge most of his damage output in the open.
