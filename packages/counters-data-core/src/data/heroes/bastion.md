## Core Playbook
Bastion holds a defensible angle in Sentry configuration to shred anyone who walks into his sightline, switching to Tank configuration to reposition or clear a barrier before resetting.

## Tactical Caveats
He has almost no personal mobility, so flanking heroes or a well-timed dive that reaches him before Sentry configuration is spun up can kill him with little resistance.
