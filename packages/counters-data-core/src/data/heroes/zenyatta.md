## Core Playbook
Zenyatta applies Orb of Discord to a priority target to amplify his team's damage, balancing Orb of Destruction poke with Orb of Harmony's healing from a safe distance.

## Tactical Caveats
He has the lowest health pool among supports and no mobility tool, so any flanker who reaches him without being intercepted almost always gets a free kill.
