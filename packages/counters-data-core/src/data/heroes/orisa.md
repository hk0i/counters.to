## Core Playbook
Orisa leads with Fortify active to shrug off crowd control, using Energy Javelin to peel dive threats off her supports and Terra Surge to force the enemy off a contested point.

## Tactical Caveats
Her damage output is low relative to other brawl tanks, so she relies on teammates to convert the space she creates rather than winning duels on her own.
