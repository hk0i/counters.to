## Core Playbook
Vendetta closes distance with Soaring Slice and Whirlwind Dash to engage in melee range, using Warding Stance to block incoming damage and Onslaught's speed bonus to chain kills together.

## Tactical Caveats
Warding Stance only blocks frontal damage and drains her energy, so flanking her or catching her from behind while she's engaged with another target removes her only defensive tool.
