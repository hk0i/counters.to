## Core Playbook
Soldier: 76 lays down consistent pressure with Heavy Pulse Rifle fire, dropping Biotic Field to sustain himself and nearby allies while Sprint repositions him to a better angle.

## Tactical Caveats
He has no burst damage or hard escape tool, so a coordinated dive that catches him without Sprint or Biotic Field up will usually kill him before he can fight back.
