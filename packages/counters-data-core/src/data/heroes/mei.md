## Core Playbook
Mei uses Ice Wall to split the enemy team or block a dive before freezing an isolated target with Endothermic Blaster for a guaranteed follow-up kill.

## Tactical Caveats
Her weapon has almost no effective range, so poke comps that never let her close the distance can kite her indefinitely.
