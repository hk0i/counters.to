## Core Playbook
Brigitte stays glued to her frontline, using Shield Bash to stop a diver mid-engage and Repair Pack to keep her tank topped off during extended trades.

## Tactical Caveats
Her healing output is low compared to dedicated healers and she has almost no range, so she contributes little if her team isn't actively brawling at close quarters.
