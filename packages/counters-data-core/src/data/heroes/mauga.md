## Core Playbook
Mauga charges into the middle of a fight with Cardiac Overdrive active, using the lifesteal from his dual miniguns to out-sustain the enemy front line while Berserker denies incoming crowd control.

## Tactical Caveats
Berserker only protects him during the ultimate window; outside of it he has no barrier and no mobility tool, so a coordinated burst combo before he engages can drop him quickly.
