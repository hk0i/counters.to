## Core Playbook
Baptiste alternates between damage and healing rounds on Biotic Launcher, dropping Immortality Field to prevent a wipe and Exo Boots to reposition to a safer angle.

## Tactical Caveats
Immortality Field is a single fixed point that enemies can play around or bait out early, and once it's down he has no other answer to a follow-up burst combo.
