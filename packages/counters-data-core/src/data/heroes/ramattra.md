## Core Playbook
Ramattra pokes from range with Void Accretion in his default form, then switches to Nemesis Form to block incoming fire with Annihilation and punch his way through anyone who pushes into his team.

## Tactical Caveats
Nemesis Form drains on a timer regardless of whether he's using it effectively, so poor cooldown timing leaves him stuck back in the fragile ranged form during a crucial fight.
