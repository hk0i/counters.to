## Core Playbook
Junker Queen throws Jagged Blade to pull a target in or set up a follow-up throw, then leans on Adrenaline Rush's wound-based lifesteal to win extended trades at point-blank range.

## Tactical Caveats
Her sustain depends entirely on landing damage; against poke comps that never let her into melee range, Adrenaline Rush heals almost nothing.
