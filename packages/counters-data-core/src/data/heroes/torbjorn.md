## Core Playbook
Torbjörn places his Turret to lock down a chokepoint or flank route, alternating between Rivet Gun poke at range and close-range Forge Hammer swings when enemies get too close.

## Tactical Caveats
The turret is a stationary, fragile investment that any dive hero can destroy in a couple of hits, and Torbjörn himself has no mobility tool to escape once he's found.
