## Core Playbook
Illari plants Captive Sun on a sightline to heal allies passing through its beam, using Outburst to reposition and Sunstruck to punish anyone who stands still under focused fire.

## Tactical Caveats
Her healing is a stationary beam that enemies can simply walk out of, so against a mobile enemy team her sustain output drops well below other support options.
