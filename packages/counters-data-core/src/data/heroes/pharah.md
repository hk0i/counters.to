## Core Playbook
Pharah uses Jump Jet and Hover to stay airborne out of melee range, raining down Rocket Launcher splash damage on grouped enemies below.

## Tactical Caveats
She's highly visible and has a large hurtbox in the air, making her one of the most reliable targets for a dedicated hitscan hero like Ashe or Soldier: 76 to shoot down.
