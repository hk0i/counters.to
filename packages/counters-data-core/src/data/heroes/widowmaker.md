## Core Playbook
Widowmaker holds a long sightline from an elevated position, using Grappling Hook to reposition between angles and Venom Mine to deny flanks while she lines up one-shot headshots.

## Tactical Caveats
She has almost no defense at close range, so any flanker who reaches her before she can reposition will win the fight nearly every time.
