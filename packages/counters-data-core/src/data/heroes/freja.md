## Core Playbook
Freja sticks explosive bolts to targets from range with Take Aim, using Quick Dash to reposition and Updraft to knock enemies out of position before finishing with a headshot.

## Tactical Caveats
Her Revdraw Crossbow has real travel time and a reload window, so hitscan duelists who catch her mid-reload or mid-dash punish her before she can reset.
