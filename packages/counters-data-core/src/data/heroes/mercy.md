## Core Playbook
Mercy pockets a high-value damage dealer with Caduceus Staff's damage boost, using Guardian Angel to reposition to safety and Resurrect to undo a bad trade the moment it's safe to do so.

## Tactical Caveats
She has no way to fight back at range and depends entirely on line of sight to her healing target, so any flanker who isolates her from her pocket target can kill her almost for free.
