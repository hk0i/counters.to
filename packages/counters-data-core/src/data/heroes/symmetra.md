## Core Playbook
Symmetra sets up Sentry Turrets and Teleporter around a choke point, using her charging beam to melt shields and grouped enemies who linger in the area she controls.

## Tactical Caveats
Her beam has very short range and no falloff protection, so she's almost entirely reliant on the enemy team pushing into her setup rather than being able to create pressure herself.
