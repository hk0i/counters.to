## Core Playbook
Zarya bubbles herself or a diving teammate right as burst damage lands, then converts the resulting energy into a high-damage beam that melts through barriers and grouped enemies alike.

## Tactical Caveats
Her damage output is directly tied to bubble timing; if she can't find a reason to pop a bubble, her beam and Graviton Surge both do far less work than they should.
