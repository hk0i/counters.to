## Core Playbook
Sierra marks a target with Tracking Shot so her Helix Rifle fire follows them automatically, using Anchor Drone to reposition or close distance before finishing with Trailblazer.

## Tactical Caveats
Her damage output depends on keeping a mark active on one target; against comps that break line of sight constantly, she loses her core damage amplifier.
