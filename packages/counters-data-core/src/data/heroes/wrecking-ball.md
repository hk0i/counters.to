## Core Playbook
Wrecking Ball grapples into the enemy backline to trigger Minefield or Piledriver, scattering the enemy formation so his own team can clean up the disorganized fight that follows.

## Tactical Caveats
His value is almost entirely disruption rather than personal kill power, so against a team that just regroups after the initial scatter, he contributes little sustained pressure.
