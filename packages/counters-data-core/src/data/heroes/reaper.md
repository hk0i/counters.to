## Core Playbook
Reaper uses Wraith Form or Shadow Step to get in close undetected, then leans on The Reaping's lifesteal to win point-blank shotgun trades against grouped enemies.

## Tactical Caveats
His shotguns fall off hard at range, so poke comps and long sightlines neutralize him almost completely unless he can close the distance first.
