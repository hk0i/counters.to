## Core Playbook
Genji dashes into the backline with Swift Strike, using Deflect to neutralize incoming projectiles or ultimates before cutting down priority targets with shuriken and sword combos.

## Tactical Caveats
Deflect does nothing against hitscan or beam damage, so heroes like Soldier: 76 or Zarya can punish an aggressive Genji without him having a defensive answer.
