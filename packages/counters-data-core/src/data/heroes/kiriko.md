## Core Playbook
Kiriko teleports to her tank via Swift Step to keep pace with a dive, cleansing crowd control with Protection Suzu and healing through Ofuda while looking for an opening to finish a weakened enemy with Kunai.

## Tactical Caveats
Wall Climb and Swift Step both put her in exposed positions between cooldowns, and she has no barrier, so sustained hitscan pressure can burn her down quickly.
