## Core Playbook
Sojourn builds Power Slide charge while peppering enemies with Railgun's automatic fire, cashing it in for an armor-piercing shot that punishes anyone caught out of cover.

## Tactical Caveats
Her charged shot requires sustained uptime on target to build, so heroes who deny sightlines or force her to reposition constantly starve her of her main source of burst.
