## Core Playbook
Hanzo holds an elevated sightline to threaten one-shot picks with Storm Arrow bursts or a fully-drawn shot, using Lunge to reposition between angles.

## Tactical Caveats
His arrows have significant travel time and drop, so fast-moving flankers and hitscan heroes at close range consistently beat him in a straight fight.
