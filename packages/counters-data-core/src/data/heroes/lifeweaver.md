## Core Playbook
Lifeweaver heals from range with Healing Blossom, using Life Grip to pull an endangered ally to safety and Petal Platform to control high ground and sightlines for his team.

## Tactical Caveats
Life Grip can be baited or used on the wrong target under pressure, and once it's on cooldown he has no other hard save for a teammate about to die.
