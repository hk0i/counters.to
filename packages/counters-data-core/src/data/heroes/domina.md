## Core Playbook
Domina holds a long sightline behind her segmented Barrier Array, charging Photon Magnum for high-impact pokes while Sonic Repulsors punish anyone who tries to close the gap.

## Tactical Caveats
She has almost nothing for enemies who are already on top of her; a flanker or dive tank that closes distance before the barrier segments are broken can bypass her entire kit.
