## Core Playbook
Emre strafes at close-to-mid range with his burst rifle, swapping to the lifesteal pistol of Siphon Blaster to win extended duels and Cyber Frag to finish off anyone who tries to retreat.

## Tactical Caveats
His sustain comes entirely from landing shots on enemies, so against a team that plays around cover and denies him clean sightlines, he loses his main survivability tool.
