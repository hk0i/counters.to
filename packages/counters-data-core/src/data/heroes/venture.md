## Core Playbook
Venture burrows underground with Drill Dash to bypass barriers and chokepoints entirely, surfacing behind the enemy line to detonate Smart Excavator charges at close range.

## Tactical Caveats
Burrowing telegraphs their general direction and they have no ranged option once they surface, so a team that tracks the burrow and focuses them on emergence punishes the dive hard.
