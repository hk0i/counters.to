## Core Playbook
Ashe holds a mid-range sightline with the Viper's scoped shots, using Coach Gun to create separation and B.O.B. to force a fight on her terms.

## Tactical Caveats
Her scoped fire rate is slow relative to other hitscan duelists, so flankers who close distance before she can land a headshot often win the exchange outright.
