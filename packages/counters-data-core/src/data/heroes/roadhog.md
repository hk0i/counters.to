## Core Playbook
Roadhog holds an angle and lands Chain Hook on an isolated target, following up with a point-blank Scrap Gun blast before retreating to heal off the trade with Take a Breather.

## Tactical Caveats
He has no barrier and limited team utility beyond the hook pick, so if the hook whiffs he's committed his cooldown and is exposed as one of the squishier tanks in a straight fight.
