## Core Playbook
Cassidy rolls to reposition and reload before landing a Magnetic Grenade to strip armor and shields, then closes out the trade with high-damage Peacekeeper fanning.

## Tactical Caveats
His combo requires landing shots at range without cover; against dive comps that close distance before he can roll away, he has little defense beyond his own aim.
