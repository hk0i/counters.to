## Core Playbook
Anran uses Inferno Rush to close on an isolated backline target, stacking burn from her Zhuque Fans before finishing with Fan the Flames, and can cheat death once per fight with Vermillion Revival.

## Tactical Caveats
Vermillion Revival is a one-time safety net on a long cooldown; once it's spent she's as fragile as any other flanker and has no other escape tool.
