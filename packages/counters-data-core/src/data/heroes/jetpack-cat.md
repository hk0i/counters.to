## Core Playbook
Jetpack Cat stays airborne with permanent flight to heal allies from safe angles with Purr and Biotic Pawjectiles, using Lifeline to tow an ally out of danger before it becomes fatal.

## Tactical Caveats
Constant flight makes her a highly visible silhouette with no barrier or shield, and hitscan heroes who track her in the air can punish her positioning severely.
