## Core Playbook
Ana holds a safe angle and lands Biotic Rifle shots to heal allies or chip enemy health, saving Sleep Dart to stop a dive or Biotic Grenade to shut down enemy sustain before a big fight.

## Tactical Caveats
Her kit is entirely projectile-based with travel time, so heroes who close distance quickly can pressure her before she gets a chance to use her utility at all.
