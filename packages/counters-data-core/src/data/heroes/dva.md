## Core Playbook
D.Va uses Boosters to fly ahead of her team and force cooldowns out of the backline, then Defense Matrix to erase the burst damage or ultimates that would normally punish an overextension.

## Tactical Caveats
Her mech has no ammo-independent sustain once Defense Matrix is on cooldown, and a hard stun or sleep during that window turns her dive into a free kill for the enemy backline.
