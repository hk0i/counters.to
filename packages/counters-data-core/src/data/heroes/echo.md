## Core Playbook
Echo flies around a flank to threaten the backline with Sticky Bombs and Focusing Beam, using Duplicate on a high-value target to briefly borrow their kit for a fight-winning burst.

## Tactical Caveats
She has no self-sustain and dies quickly to focused hitscan fire; a whiffed Duplicate on the wrong target wastes her ultimate almost entirely.
