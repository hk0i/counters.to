## Core Playbook
Hazard uses Jagged Wall to cut off an angle or trap enemies against it, then Violent Leap to close the remaining distance and knock a priority target into his kill zone.

## Tactical Caveats
His wall and leap are both single-target commitments with real cooldowns; whiffing either against a team that just walks around the wall leaves him without a follow-up.
