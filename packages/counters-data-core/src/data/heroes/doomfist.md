## Core Playbook
Doomfist chains Seismic Slam, Rocket Punch, and Power Block to close distance instantly and isolate a single backline target, converting punch damage directly into shields for himself.

## Tactical Caveats
Every engage commits all of his mobility at once; if the punch is blocked or he's rooted out of the air, he has no fallback and is one of the easiest tanks in the game to burst down.
