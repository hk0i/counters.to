## Core Playbook
Winston leaps onto backline targets with Jump Pack and drops a Barrier Projector to isolate them from the rest of the fight, using Tesla Cannon's beam to punish anyone who tries to burst him down at close range.

## Tactical Caveats
He has no mobility tool once Jump Pack is on cooldown, and shotgun or melee heroes who trade with him in the pocket he creates can kill him before his barrier timer runs out.
