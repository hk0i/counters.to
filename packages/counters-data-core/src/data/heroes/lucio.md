## Core Playbook
Lúcio wall-rides around the map to stay unpredictable, switching between Speed Boost to enable a dive and Healing Boost to sustain his team through a fight, with Sound Barrier to prevent a wipe.

## Tactical Caveats
His healing output per target is low compared to dedicated healers, so a team relying on him as a primary healer against high burst damage will often lose the trade.
